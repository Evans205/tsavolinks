Company Overview

Tsavolinks Consulting

Tsavolinks Consulting is a premier investment and insurance/risk management advisory firm headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. With a strong footprint in Kenya and the East African region, we specialize in delivering expert guidance and strategic solutions in protection planning, asset management, and retirement planning for both individuals and corporates.

We are proudly associated with top-tier insurance providers and leading asset management firms, enabling us to offer our clients best-in-class products and services across the financial landscape.
